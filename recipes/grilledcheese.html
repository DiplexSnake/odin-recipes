Cheddar Grilled Cheese

Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side-down onto skillet bottom and add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side-up on top of sandwich. Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted.

Ingredients

    2 slices bread
  1. 2 slices cheddar cheese
  2. Butter
